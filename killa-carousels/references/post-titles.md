# 265 Clickable Post Title Templates

Organized by content category from High-Selling Content Buckets. Most can be mixed and matched across categories. Goal: give you IDEAS, not restrictions. Play with wording to find what sounds best.

---

## ⚠️ READ BEFORE USING THESE TEMPLATES

The 265 templates below are starting points — they only work if filled in with the right voice. Most titles Claude writes go wrong not because the *template* is wrong, but because the *filling* is wrong.

### The Non-Negotiable Title Rules (full version in SKILL.md, Step 5)

1. **5–8 words ideal. 10 words is the hard ceiling.** Count them.
2. **Zero jargon.** A non-expert in the topic must understand every word. "Core Web Vitals" → "Why your site feels slow."
3. **Curiosity gap, not content delivery.** The title creates the pull. The slides deliver the answer.
4. **End with » or →** when pulling into slide 2. Skip if the title is already a question.
5. **Italicize the punch word** — the one word carrying the emotional weight. "How to sell *without* sounding salesy »"
6. **Sound like a sentence said out loud**, not a blog post headline.
7. **Kill AI-giveaway language**: em dashes inside titles, "leverage," "unlock," "discover," "ultimate guide," "X ways to Y."
8. **Confession beats instruction** when the topic allows. "I failed X on purpose" outperforms "5 lessons from failing at X."

### Examples of the Voice You're Aiming At

Every template you pick should produce something that sounds like these:

- *"I failed a client project on purpose »"*
- *"WTF even is an email funnel?"*
- *"How to sell without sounding salesy »"*
- *"Can you spot the problem with this headline? »"*
- *"Our Instagram launch failed. Here's why »"*
- *"I wrote ads for Oatly and they failed »"*
- *"The truth about ranking on the 1st page of Google?"*
- *"Do you get annoyed when your kid won't eat their dinner? Read this →"*
- *"Steal these 109 email subject lines"*
- *"20 ways to say join our email list"*
- *"Funnels are dead. Sales engines are in."*

### When Filling Templates — Common Errors to Avoid

| Template | ❌ Wrong filling | ✅ Right filling |
|---|---|---|
| "The truth about [topic]" | "The truth about generative engine optimization strategies" | "The truth about ranking on Google" |
| "How to [verb] [goal]" | "How to leverage AI search to maximize content visibility" | "How to make AI quote your content" |
| "Why [advice] is the problem" | "Why over-relying on outdated SEO tactics is the problem" | "Why your SEO advice is outdated" |
| "X [tips] you need to know" | "5 powerful on-page SEO optimization strategies you need to know" | "5 SEO fixes that take 10 minutes" |
| "Stop [action]" | "Stop neglecting your Core Web Vitals optimization" | "Stop ignoring how slow your site feels" |
