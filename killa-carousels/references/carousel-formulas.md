## EDUCATE FORMULA 1 — Actionable Tips

**Slide 1: Clickable post title**
- [Struggling] [to/with] [achieve goal]?
- X [actionable tips] you [should/need to] know
- How to [action verb] [end goal] →

**Slide 2: Give beginner context / Distill topic (optional)**
- You may have heard…
- You already know…
- New [people] always ask me…
- Let's talk about…
- You've gotta start…
- You need [solution].
- It's probably because…
- ICYMI / In case you missed it —

**Slides 3-8: Actionable tips**
- [Take this action.]
- + {Give a description, add examples, and/or offer additional tips that would help them complete the action more effectively.}

**Slide 9: Big takeaway (optional)**
- Remember: …
- Stop {taking this action} and start {taking this action instead}.
- If you want to {get this result}, you need to {take this action}.
- It's not about {this action}. It's about {this one}.
- (Your/The way you) {action} matters.
- The (purpose/goal) of {taking this action} isn't to {get this result}. It's to {get this result}.
- {Taking action} isn't the only way to {achieve goal}.
- {Goal} doesn't just happen. You need to {take this action}.

**Slide 10: CTA**
- Use a Plug-and-Play CTA from the CTA library.

## EDUCATE FORMULA 3 — Value Share / Actionable Tips

**Slide 1: Clickable post title**
- The truth about [topic].
- [Topic], finally explained.
- What no one tells you about [topic]

**Slide 2: A fact or statistic**
- Did you know…
- Fact: …
- According to…
- {State the fact}.
Note: Choose your MESSAGE first, then find the best statistic. If you want the number to seem big, use percentages (80% > 4 out of 5). If small, use fractions (1 out of 4 > 25%).

**Slide 3: A truth, revealed**
- {Achieving goal} isn't about {this}. It's about {that}.
- So clearly, {takeaway from the statistic}.
- So if {experience they're having}, {what they need to know}.
- {Spill the truth}. So if {you're not taking this action}, {how they're missing out}.
- Which is why {what the fact explains}.

**Slide 4: Big promise / Topic distilled**
- Here's how to…
- Let's talk about…
- So, what SHOULD you {do}? {Take this action.}
- You need [step or solution] if you want to {get result}.
- {Reaching your goal} won't {look like this}. It will {look like this}.
- That's why {action} is {necessary}.

**Slides 5-8: Examples / Actionable tips**
- For example…
- Instead of…try…
- #X: {Take this action}
- Next time {you approach topic}, ask yourself…
Note: Think of tips as BEFORE and AFTER pairs so good examples can be compared to bad ones.

**Slide 9: Big takeaway (optional)**
- Same prompts as Educate Formula 1, Slide 9

**Slide 10: CTA**

**Slide 1: Clickable post title**

**Slide 2: Breakdown of plug-and-play categories (optional)**
- If you're a…{list identities}

- For...{list categories}

## EDUCATE FORMULA 5 — Problem Awareness

**Slide 1: Clickable post title**
- [Common advice] is [overrated/unnecessary/a waste of time/etc.].
- Should you [take this action]?
- Why [action/product] [could be/might/can] [having negative effect] →

**Slide 2: The real problem**
- If you find yourself {taking these actions}, you could be {experiencing this unintentional consequence.}
- How do you {get this result} without {this consequence}?
- Do you ever find yourself wondering {big question they're struggling to answer}?
- Every {audience} knows {shared problem experience}.
- You're {doing these things "right"}. So why are you still {struggling}?
- You're ready to {reach this goal}. But {big question}?
- Are you {how you're unintentionally contributing to problem}?
Note: The problem usually ISN'T what people think. Focus on how the AUDIENCE is contributing to the problem.

**Slide 3: Spill the truth / Offer a perspective shift**
- The truth is…
- Think of it this way:
- It seems like {what they think is the problem}. But {what the real problem is}.
- What if {new truth they hadn't considered}?

**Slide 4: Back up your argument**
- {Reaching goal} doesn't look like {X}. It looks like {Y}.
- Every time you {take action}, you might be {seeing result}. But you're also {negative impact}.
- When you {take action}, {invisible negative impact}.

**Slide 5: Make a connection / Empathize**
- I get it. You want… But…
- I see so many {audience} struggle with this. Because…
- And it makes sense. You've been taught {false promise}.
Note: This slide can come earlier (after slide 2) if the topic is sensitive.

**Slide 6: The big lesson**
- But if you want {different result}, {what they need to do differently}.
- But {result} doesn't have to {come at the expense of negative consequence}.

**Slide 7: Possible solutions**
- So instead of {continuing previous action with poor results}, try {alternative course of action}.
- Here's how to {start doing things differently} →
- Next time you want to {approach topic / achieve result}... Here's how to do it →
Note: These slides are optional. If no plug-and-play examples exist, you could offer your product/service as the solution instead.

**Slide 8: Examples**
- Instead of…Try…
- #X: {Take this action}

**Slide 9: Big takeaway (optional)**
- Same prompts as Educate Formula 1, Slide 9

**Slide 10: CTA**

## RELATE FORMULA 1 — Reflections & Recaps

**Slide 1: Clickable post title**
- Why I'm/we're switching [to Y / from X to Y]
- We/I [took this action]. Here's what happened.
- How [my or our business/deliverable] [achieves result]

**Slide 2: The backstory**
- (In {year} / X years ago), I was…when {this happened}
- Back in {month, year}, I started…
- Flashback to {year}: {describe how things were going right}
- We've been {doing things this way}... Now, we're {name the change}. Here's why…
- I've been {taking this action} for {timeframe}.

**Slide 3: Your big problem**
- But here's the problem:
- The only problem was…
- But after {X} happened, we {experienced this problem}.
- {List the problems you're experiencing}

**Slide 4: Agitate your problem**
- And on top of that…
- Since I had {circumstances that made it difficult}, I knew {it was time for change}.
- At this point, I {circumstances that made it difficult}.

**Slide 5: Your big solution**
- Enter: {solution}.
- After {action you needed to make decision}, I finally…
- Which is why I decided to…
- So I started {pursuing solution}.

**Slides 6-8: The results of implementing the solution**
- Here's what happened next…
- The results?
- My {result} {skyrocketed/increased/decreased} to {#}.
- Pretty much right away, I {started seeing results}.
- And ever since {pursuing solution}, I've been {emotion}.

**Slide 9: The big reflection / Recap**
- All in all, I {big result you got}.
- I realized it's not about {this action}. It's about {this one}.
- So maybe this is your sign to {pursue solution, too}.
- If you've been waiting to {pursue solution, too}, go for it.

**Slide 10: CTA**

## RELATE FORMULA 2 — Story Shares

**Slide 1: Clickable post title**
- I/we [took difficult or surprising action.]
- My/our [first try] [didn't do well].
- I/we [stopped] [negative action/behavior] →

**Slide 2: A moment from the CLIMAX of your story**
- I was {in the middle of experience} when {climactic moment}.
- I was {describe what it looked like in the middle of the climactic experience}. (Ex: I was crying at my kitchen table at 2AM, rereading the email for the 3rd time.)
- "Climactic dialogue." (Ex: "I want a divorce.")
- It all started when {client} said, "Climactic dialogue."
Note: Start with the CLIMAX, not the beginning. This builds instant momentum.

**Slide 3: The backstory**
- It was {year} and I was…{describe the problematic situation}.
- Back in {month, year}, I'd started…but…
- I've been {taking this action} for {timeframe}, and {describe the problem}.

**Slide 4: The moment of solution / Transformation**
- And that's when I realized…
- Suddenly, it hit me:
- But then something happened that would change my life forever…
- But I was {missing the most important part}: …

**Slide 5: Distill the "aha" moment**
- I realized it's not about {this action}. It's about {this one}.
- Because {goal} doesn't just happen. You need to {take this action}.
- The next time {you're faced with experience}, ask yourself…
- I didn't need to {action you thought you needed to take}. I needed to…

**Slides 6-9: Lessons from the big takeaway**
- The big takeaways? {List 3 takeaways}.
- If I could start over and do it again?
- The moral of the story?
- If you're in the same spot? Here's what to do…
- Here are {X changes} I made as a result…
- Now, my {life/business/etc.} looks like…

**Slide 10: CTA**

## SELL FORMULA 1 — The Only Option

**Slide 1: Clickable post title**
- The problem with [alternate solution].
- Why [other action] failed [optional: you].
- Why [other action] [doesn't work].

**Slide 2: Context**
- Here's a mistake I see so many {clients/customers} make:
- People often ask me {isn't (alternative)} {a better/equally good solution}?
- Every time I see people {seek out alternative solution}, I think, {relatable phrase}.

**Slide 3: Why it seems like a good alternative**
- I know {alternative offer} seems like {a possibility}
- When you hear {alternative offer} can get {great results, for less work/investment} it seems like {it's worth a try}
- I get it — {alternative} seems like {a great solution} because…
- There's no question {alternative} is a great option if you're/if you want…{List why}

**Slide 4: Why it's not a good alternative (big picture)**
- And it can be…IF {you meet qualifications}.
- But if you're {describe the audience}, then…
- The problem is: {describe the problem with alternative offers}.
- But I know that even if {alternative} gives you {short-term solutions}, it {will fall short later}

**Slide 5: Agitate why it's not a good alternative**
- Which is why {specific ways people don't find success with alternatives}.
- Which is why {you hear these specific complaints about alternatives}.
- And {ways alternative solutions can make the problem worse}.

**Slide 6: What the alternative is missing**
- Because if you want {what real results look like}, you need {what your offer gets right that others get wrong}.
- Because {alternative} doesn't {take this important factor into account}.
- Because {alternative} is {missing this fundamental element}.

**Slide 7: The only option — YOUR offer**
- {Your offer} is the only {service/product} that will {give you these specific benefits}.
- {Offer} is specifically {designed/set up} to…
- I made {offer} to give you {this big benefit}...
- I made {offer} because I saw so many people struggling with {problem}...

**Slide 8: Path they could keep pursuing**
- You could keep…
- If you're {identity reasons to seek alternatives}
- If you want {valid reasons to seek out alternatives first}

**Slide 9: Path they should start pursuing**
- Or you could…
- But if you're {identity reasons you're the best fit}
- But if you want {results your offer does better}

**Slide 10: CTA**

## SELL FORMULA 3 — Self-Selection

**Slide 1: Clickable post title**
- If you're (optional: a {job title/niche}) struggling to [achieve this desired result]...
- If you've been waiting to [try/start]...
- When you want [X] but don't have [Y]

**Slide 2: Direct sell of your offer**
- Stop {taking this action} and {try this instead}
- You need to try {offer}.
- {This offer} is (optional: perfect/made/just/etc.) for you.
- I'm {Name}, and I {what you teach/offer, concisely}.
- Don't {take alternative action}. Try {your offer}.

**Slide 3: Introduce your offer**
- {My offer} {gives/teaches/ etc.} you {exactly what they need} {even if you don't have/without/etc.}.
- {Offer} is a {service/product type} for {audience} that {describe the big benefit}.
- This is what {an offer that gives them results} looks like…

**Slides 3-9: List benefits of individual features/deliverables**
- {Optional: It's got} {feature} to {give you this benefit}.
- {This feature/detail} is designed to {give you this benefit}.
- You'll get {deliverable} so you can {get this benefit}.
- You'll get {deliverable} to help you {avoid this problem}.
- There are {describe elements} to make it {easier/faster/more affordable} than ever to {get this benefit}.
- And with X {features}, you'll never {face this problem} again.
- And it also comes with {additional feature} so you can {get this benefit}.

**Slide 10: CTA**
