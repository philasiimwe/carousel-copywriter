# Plug-and-Play CTAs Reference

A CTA (call-to-action) is a simple, clear, and SPECIFIC instruction about what you want your audience to do next. Use a CTA at the end of every post. Structure: Primary Lead-In + Primary CTA. Optionally add a Secondary Lead-In + Secondary CTA for engagement.

---

## CTA FORMULA OPTIONS

### Option 1: Primary CTA Only (most posts)
**Primary Lead-In** → **Primary CTA**

### Option 2: Primary + Secondary CTA (for engagement or special offer reminders)
**Primary Lead-In** → **Primary CTA** → **Secondary Lead-In** → **Secondary CTA**

---

## PRIMARY LEAD-INS

### "Want…?" Lead-Ins
- Want to learn more about [topic]?
- Want more [benefit] in your life?
- Want to steal my [details of offer]?
- Want [the/my] FULL [details of offer]?
- Want all [the/my] [deliverables]?
- Want to take [undesirable task] permanently off your hands?
- And if you never want to [do this undesirable task] again?
- And if you never want to [experience this undesirable feeling/result] again?
- And when you get to the point [when/where] [experience]?
- And when you start to think, "[Realization you need help]"...

### "Ready to…?" Lead-Ins
- Ready to say goodbye to [negative experience]?
- Ready to say hello to [positive outcome]?
- Ready to start [taking action]?
- Ready to stop [negative behavior, action, or inaction]?
- Ready to [experience a positive outcome]?

### "So here's…" / "So let's…" Lead-Ins
- So here's your [starting point/first step/next step/invitation/etc.].
- So let's [go after results].
- So let's [do it together].
- So here's what to do [next time/when] [you have experience].

### "Sound like…?" Lead-Ins
- Sound like just the thing you've been waiting for?
- Sound like exactly what you need?
- Sound like you?
- Sound familiar?
- Sound like a plan?
- Sound right up your alley?

### "Need…?" Lead-Ins
- Need to [do this undesirable task] but don't know where to begin?
- Need [help] [making it happen]?
- And if you need a [noun]?
- And if you need it all [done for you]?
- And if you need more [what they need before they can start]?
- And if you need [benefit/result] ASAP?
- And if you need [benefit/result], but don't know [how to get/do it]?
- And if you need [outcome], like, yesterday?

### "And if you're thinking…" Lead-Ins
- "I'm totally new to [topic]. Where do I even begin?"
- "How can I [get these results for myself]?"
- "What about [related problem/product/service]?"
- "What if I [add objection]?"
- "If only I could [get this positive outcome]..."
- "If only you had [name or describe the offer]..."
- "Sure, it worked for you. But what about me?"
- "Who has time for all that?!"
- "That sounds…hard."
- "Can you take this off my hands?"
- "Is there an easier way to do this?"
- "So…what's next?"
- "Yup, that sounds like me…"
- "I've gotta know more."

### Transition Lead-Ins
- Turn your [before] into [after].
- Your [transformation] is waiting.
- Let's make it happen.
- So why wait?
- So what are you waiting for?
- I already know what you're thinking.
- And before you ask…
- Don't worry. I've got you covered.

## CTAs BY OFFER TYPE

### 1-to-1 Service

**Schedule a session:**
- Let's get you on my calendar!
- My calendar is open.
- Fill out my inquiry form, and I'll send you a link to my calendar.
- Book a [name of session] session with me!
- Book your first [topic] appointment.

**Direct to DMs:**
- Send us a DM and tell us [what you need to know]!
- Drop us a DM!
- Hit us up in the DMs.
- Full permission to slide into our DMs.
- Our DMs are open! See you there.

**Inquire/Apply:**
- Fill out a quick inquiry form on my website!
- Apply to work with me at the link in my bio.

**Full Pitch CTA Template:**
"You need [name of 1-1 service]. In [this amount of time], you'll [take this action] and I'll [give you this deliverable]. [Tell them the action to sign up.]"

"It's time [for/to] [a transformation]. [Direct action to take] and we'll [what they can expect]. You'll leave with [what they'll walk away with.] [Final result], here you come."

### Product Shop
- Shop all [products] at the link in my bio!
- Shop the new [product] drop at…
- See the whole collection at…
- Find your perfect fit at…
- Check out [my/our] full shop of [products].

- Invite [us/me] into your inbox.

### Quiz

## ENGAGEMENT CTAs

### Comments (With Automation)
- Comment [KEYWORD] below if you want to learn more
- Comment [KEYWORD] and I'll send you a link to snag it
- Drop a [EMOJI] if you want to try this out for yourself

### Comments (Without Automation)
**Clarifying:** Drop your questions about (topic) in the comments below!
**Connection:** Who else loves [niche topic]?! / Are you struggling with [this problem], too?
**Prompting:** Which one is your favorite? / Drop your fav from the list! / Which [topic] should I [action] next?

### Likes, Follows, Shares, Saves
- SCROLL (to the end) to see [content/teaser]
- Like, save, and share if you wanna see more like this!
- SAVE THIS POST for the next time you need [what it delivers].
- SHARE this post with someone who [is in this situation / needs it].
- Tag a friend who needs to hear this!
- Got a [bestie/coworker/partner] who needs this? Tag 'em.
